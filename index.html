About Our Farm

Discover Our Peaceful Retreat

Nestled in the picturesque countryside of Phaltan, Satara, Kadam Farms offers a perfect blend of rural charm and modern comfort. Our farmhouse spans over 5 acres of lush greenery, providing a tranquil escape from city life.

Established in 2010, we've been hosting guests who seek authentic farm experiences, organic food, and a chance to reconnect with nature. Our farm produces seasonal fruits, vegetables, and dairy products that you can enjoy during your stay.

Whether you're looking for a weekend getaway, a family vacation, or a venue for special events, Kadam Farms provides the perfect setting with its serene atmosphere and warm hospitality.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Kadam Farms suitable for families with children?

Absolutely! We have special activities designed for kids and families, including animal feeding, nature walks, and traditional games. Our cottages are family-friendly with extra bedding available.

Do you provide vegetarian or vegan food?

Yes, we specialize in farm-fresh vegetarian cuisine with many vegan options available. All meals are prepared with organic ingredients grown on our farm or sourced locally.

Is there parking available?

Yes, we offer free and secure parking on the premises with shaded areas to protect your vehicle from the sun. The parking area is just a short walk from the cottages.

How can I book my stay?

You can book directly through our website contact form, call us at +91 7875707766, or message us on WhatsApp. We recommend booking at least 2 weeks in advance for weekends.

What should I pack for my farm stay?

We recommend comfortable cotton clothing, sturdy shoes for farm walks, hats/sunscreen, insect repellent, and a camera to capture the beautiful rural scenery. Nights can be cool, so bring a light jacket.

Farm Blog

Monsoon Magic at Kadam Farms

June 2025

This monsoon season has transformed our fields into a lush green paradise! Our guests enjoyed special mango picking experiences, adventurous bullock cart rides through the rain-washed trails, and cozy evenings by the fireplace with our organic farm-to-table meals. The frogs are singing, the peacocks are dancing, and the entire farm is alive with activity. Check out our Instagram for stunning photos of the dramatic monsoon skies over our farmland!

Our New Organic Kitchen Garden

May 2025

We've expanded our kitchen garden with over 20 new varieties of vegetables, herbs, and edible flowers! Now guests can join us in morning harvest sessions where we pick fresh ingredients that will be used in that day's meals. We've also started weekend workshops on organic farming techniques - last Saturday we had a full house learning about companion planting and natural pest control. The new garden has already supplied over 50kg of fresh produce this month!

Traditional Harvest Festival Celebration

April 2025

We celebrated the traditional harvest festival with our guests and local farming community in grand style! The day began with a ceremonial ploughing demonstration using our farm oxen, followed by folk dances, traditional games, and a feast prepared from our winter harvest. Guests participated in grain-threshing, learned to make jaggery from sugarcane, and enjoyed folk stories by the bonfire in the evening. It was wonderful to share these rural traditions with visitors from the city.

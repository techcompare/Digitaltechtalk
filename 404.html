About The City

A Perfect Blend of
Tradition & Tech

Pune represents the perfect amalgamation of the old and the new. Once the seat of the Peshwas, today it stands tall as a major IT hub and the automobile manufacturing capital of India.

Education Hub

Known as the 'Oxford of the East', home to students from over 100 countries.

IT & Auto Giant

Hinjewadi and Magarpatta drive India's digital economy, hosting global tech giants.

Discover History

Historical Gems

Fort

Shaniwar Wada

1732 AD

The majestic fortification that served as the seat of the Peshwas of the Maratha Empire. Famous for its massive doors.

Monument

Aga Khan Palace

1892 AD

Built by Sultan Muhammed Shah Aga Khan III. It served as a prison for Mahatma Gandhi during the freedom struggle.

Trekking

Sinhagad Fort

1300m

"The Lion's Fort" offers breathtaking views of the Sahyadris and is a favorite weekend trekking spot for locals.

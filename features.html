Features

What TicketStub can do

A walkthrough of everything TicketStub offers today, and the thinking behind each feature.

01

Photo → Ticket Stub

Pick any photo from your gallery or take a new one, and TicketStub converts it into a stub-style memory card in a few taps — cropped, framed, and ready to annotate. No design skills needed; the app handles the layout.

02

Dates, Locations & Notes

Every stub can carry the context that makes a memory mean something later: the date it happened, where you were, and a short personal note. It's the difference between "a photo from sometime" and a memory you can actually place.

03

Personal Collection View

Your stubs live in a browsable collection designed to feel like flipping through a box of kept tickets — not scrolling an endless camera roll grid. Recent stubs surface naturally so your most recent memories are easy to find.

04

Search & Organize

Find a stub again by searching its title, note, or location — useful once your collection grows past the first few dozen memories.

05

Private Account

TicketStub uses simple email-and-password sign-in so your collection follows you across devices and app reinstalls, without needing to link a social account. Your stubs are tied to your account only — there's no public profile or feed.

06

Secure Cloud Sync

Stubs you create are securely synced using Supabase, so a reinstalled app or a new phone doesn't mean starting your archive over from nothing.

07

No Ads, No Clutter

TicketStub's in-app experience is currently ad-free. The interface stays focused on your stubs — no banner ads, no interstitials interrupting the flow of adding a memory.

08

Full Data Control

You can request full deletion of your account and all associated stubs at any time. See the account deletion page for the exact process.

What's next

TicketStub is actively developed. Planned additions include richer stub templates, collection sharing options, and export tools for backing up your archive outside the app. Follow progress on the Updates page.
